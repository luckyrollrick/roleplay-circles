Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

Roleplay Circles ("we," "us," or "our") is a sales roleplay matching platform that helps sales teams practice and improve. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

  • Show calendar events so you and your team know when someone's free or busy

  • Your session history and circle memberships are deleted
  • Your Google OAuth tokens are revoked and deleted
